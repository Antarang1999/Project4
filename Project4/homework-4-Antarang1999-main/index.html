Dino World: A Global Journey into the Mesozoic Era

Explore the world of dinosaurs based on where their lived. Click on a country to discover average height statistics categorized by type and the dietary preferences of dinosaurs in that region. Dive deeper by clicking on the bars to reveal detailed information about individual dinosaurs, including a URL for further exploration. Despite advancements, there's still much unknown about the lengths and diets of certain dinosaurs, adding an element of mystery to your exploration. Colour coding, tooltip, hover actions, map distribution with choropleth map are implemented along with legend for creating a good vizualization.
